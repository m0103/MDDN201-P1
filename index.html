Manhole

Manhole is the entrance to the undergroud of the city where hidden power line are located. There are different types of manhole covers that represent either power company or pipe line company.

Antenna

Antennas are used to catch signal for wireless devices. There are different forms of antenna depends on whether it is for TV, radio, internet connection and etc.

Marking

It is easy to spot markings in bright colour in Wellington street. They are usually for locating under grounf utilities.

CCTV

CCTVs are commonly used on the street or road for the public security purpose, but there are CCTVs used personal security in the place like public car park, store and apartment.
